### 🔷 LinkedIn Post (Real + Personal)

Today I worked on a **Power BI dashboard** where I tried to understand how salary data actually looks from a business point of view.

At first, it looked like just numbers… but once I started breaking it down, things became interesting.

I explored:

* Department-wise salary distribution
* Job role salaries and bonus %
* Business unit contribution
* Salary trends across different cities

One thing I noticed — salary distribution is not balanced at all.
Some roles and departments clearly dominate the cost.

Also, cities like London and Manchester showed higher totals compared to others, which made me think about how location impacts compensation.

This project helped me realize:
👉 Dashboards are not about visuals, they are about **asking the right questions**

Still learning and improving how to:
